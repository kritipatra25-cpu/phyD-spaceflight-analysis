# Methods

The investigation follows a multi-stage computational biology workflow:

1. **Differential Expression Analysis**: Identifying genes significantly perturbed under space-related stressors using NASA GeneLab pipelines.
2. **Pathway-Specific Heatmaps**: Visualizing gene clusters across biological modules (ROS, GA, Circadian, Photosynthesis) using R and Morpheus.
3. **KEGG Pathway Modeling**: Mapping differential expression onto established metabolic and signaling maps.
4. **SBGN mechanistic modeling**: Creating standardized mechanistic diagrams to describe regulatory relationships.
5. **Cross-Dataset Integration**: Identifying shared and distinct regulatory programs across microgravity, radiation, and magnetic field conditions.
6. **Biomni Synthesis**: Final integration of transcriptomic, pathway, and tissue-specific evidence into a unified germination model.
